For this project, we asked people to contribute their photos and stories via GuardianWitness, our platform for readers' content, or a secure form for those who wished to remain anonymous because of the sensitive nature of their experience. Some of the stories were told to Guardian reporters and others were submitted by organisations who work with immigrants. The stories are told in the readers' own words with some minor editing for style and the occasional addition of stock images to illustrate stories where no picture was included.
